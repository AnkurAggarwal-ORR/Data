Does winning toss related to winning?

The Kolkata Night Riders is the third-most successful team in IPL history, after the Mumbai Indians and Chennai Super Kings, according to these rounded bar graphs.

From 2008 to 2018, Mumbai Indians won 97 games, Kolkata Knight Riders won 90 games, and Chennai Super Kings won 90 games.

The two teams with the least success are Gujarat and Kochi.
