Exploring Indian Premier League with Interactive Visualizations

An overview of IPL in the last 11 years, from 2008 to 2018.

Ankur Aggarwal

Data Visualization (CS 416) , UIUC

Created in 2008, , Indian Premier League (IPL) is a professional Twenty 20 (T20) cricket tournament in India. Major Indian cities has teams in the league, which follows a round-robin group and knockout system.

The IPL data (2008-2018) is being explored utilizing  D3 to complete the visualization.

Zee Entertainment Enterprises funded the establishment of the Indian Cricket League (ICL) in 2007. The first season was supposed to premiere in April 2008 in New Delhi during a "high-profile ceremony."
